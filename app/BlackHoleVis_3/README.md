## Controls
- Hold SHIFT and...
	- Drag w. left mouse button: rotate around black hole
	- Drag w. right mouse button: reduce or increase distance to black hole
- Hold CTRL and...
	- Drag w. left mouse button: rotate camera (change view direction but keep position)
